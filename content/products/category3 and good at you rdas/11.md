The chain bushes are manufactured using Q195 steel, providing stable mechanical performance for industrial applications.

### Precision Machining

### Carburizing & Heat Treatment

Carburizing and heat treatment improve surface hardness, wear resistance, and service life.

Products are treated with oil coating or anti-rust coating to protect against corrosion during storage and transportation.
